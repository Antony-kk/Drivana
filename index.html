Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our car hire and taxi services.

How do I book a ride?

Use the "Book a Ride" button below to submit your details, and we’ll confirm your booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before your scheduled ride.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept mobile money, credit cards, and cash payments upon arrival.

Are there any additional fees for late returns?

Yes, late returns may incur an additional fee based on the hourly rate of your rental.

Can I request a specific car model?

We’ll do our best to accommodate your request, but availability may vary depending on the booking schedule.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is included, but you can opt for additional coverage for extra protection.
